Careers-Job and Internships

Career Opportunities-From PICTians, for PICTians!

Pune Institute of Computer Technology, abbreviated as PICT has been the producing engineers who have helped lead the ever developing field of technology. PICTians have made their presence felt in all fields- be it industry, research or entrepreneurship as well. PICT Alumni Network is a platform to connect all these brilliant people who were once students of this college. To relive those memories, to contribute to the institute, to share their experiences, to get to know about happenings in the alumni community and a platform to interact with the current students of the college-in short this is the platform to make you feel a part of the PICT Family!
